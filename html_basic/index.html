Is there anything called "African Strawberries"?

"African strawberries" is not a commonly recognized term for a specific type of strawberry. However, strawberries are widely cultivated in various regions of Africa, with different varieties and species grown in different parts of the continent.

Where in Africa are strawberries grown?

Strawberries are typically grown in countries with suitable climates for berry production. In Africa, you can find strawberry cultivation in countries with temperate or subtropical climates, such as South Africa, Kenya, and Egypt. It's worth noting that there are various strawberry varieties, and their flavor, size, and appearance can vary depending on the specific type and growing conditions.
